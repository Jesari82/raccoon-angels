About Us

Welcome to Raccoon Angel! We are a dedicated team of animal lovers committed to rescuing and rehabilitating raccoons in need. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for injured, orphaned, and abandoned raccoons, giving them the care and love they deserve.

At Raccoon Angel, we believe every raccoon has the right to a happy and healthy life. Our experienced team works tirelessly to ensure that each animal receives the best possible care. From medical treatment to a loving environment, we strive to prepare our raccoons for a successful release back into the wild or a safe life in sanctuary.

Join us in our mission to protect and preserve the raccoon population. Together, we can make a difference, one raccoon at a time.
